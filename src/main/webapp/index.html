Help & Support

System Documentation

Getting Started

Welcome to the Pahana Edu Online Billing System. This system helps you manage customer accounts, inventory items, and generate invoices efficiently.

User Guide

Customer Management

Add, edit, and manage customer accounts. Each customer has a unique account number.

Item Management

Manage your inventory items. Add new items, update prices, and track stock levels.

Billing System

Create invoices for customers by selecting items from inventory. The system automatically calculates totals and applies discounts.

Reports

Generate sales reports, customer reports, and inventory reports for better business insights.
